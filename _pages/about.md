Welcome! I am a data scientist applying statistical analysis and casual inference techniques to the study of procrastination in older adult populations. I am currently doing my PhD with the [SFI Center for Research Training in Foundations in Data Science](https://www.data-science.ie/the-crt-programme/) and am working in the [Maynooth University Hamilton Institute](https://www.maynoothuniversity.ie/hamilton).
